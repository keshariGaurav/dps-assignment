Principal Name: Dr. Anjali Verma
Qualification: Ph.D. in Education Management, M.Ed., B.Ed.
Experience: 18+ years in academic leadership and teaching

Profile:
Dr. Anjali Verma is an experienced and visionary educator serving as the Principal of Green Valley International School since 2018. With over 18 years of experience in the field of education, she has been instrumental in fostering a culture of academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development among students.

She strongly believes in a student-centric approach to education, integrating modern teaching methodologies with traditional values. Under her leadership, the school has achieved significant milestones in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.

Dr. Verma is passionate about nurturing young minds and encouraging innovation, critical thinking, and creativity. She actively promotes the use of technology in classrooms and ensures a safe, inclusive, and motivating environment for both students and staff.

Message from the Principal:
"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge, but about building character and shaping the future. At Green Valley International School, we strive to empower our students with the skills, values, and confidence needed to succeed in a rapidly changing world."
